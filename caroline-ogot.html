Caroline Ojenge

Founder, Director

Caroline Awuor Ojenge , an African Feminist, a women Human right's advocate. A creative poet who inspires women through her write ups, passionate about women's economic empowerment, Women's political processes and policies towards the same. Community worker whose call for safe spaces and women's organizing has achieved a lot. Women voices that cannot access the microphone or publicity have been echoed through her down up approach agitation. She is a motivational speaker, mentor and a peer educator. Caroline is a trained teacher, explains her skills and knowledge, speaks powerful from the hear. Caroline has mentored many young women who have excelled in community. A gender campaigner who has in the policy space for 5 years. Caroline was awarded a Certificate of merit by the Norwegian Embassy & Governance Forum Award in recognition of her service to humanity through her work to the internally displaced persons following the 2007 electoral violence Caroline has been a strong advocate for peace and worked to support other vulnerable populations like People Women Disabilities, Orphans and People living with HIV/AIDS. Caroline’s vision is to contribute towards building an inclusive nation that recognizes and supports human rights for ALL.
